Pa­tients in emer­gen­cy de­part­ments will be scored on the sever­i­ty of their con­di­tion to en­sure they re­ceive time­ly treat­ment, it was an­nounced yes­ter­day. A pro­ce­dure known as the emer­gen­cy medicine ear­ly warn­ing sys­tem (EMEWS) was launched yes­ter­day by Si­mon Har­ris, the health min­is­ter. Un­der the ini­tia­tive, the sever­i­ty of a pa­tient’s ail­ment will be scored and their rank­ing will prompt nurs­es to re­quest a re­view at agreed in­ter­vals while the pa­tient is wait­ing to be seen by a doc­tor. The sys­tem is in­tend­ed to de­tect a pa­tient’s con­di­tion be­fore it wors­ens.
